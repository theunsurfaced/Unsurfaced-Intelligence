# DECLASSIFIED CULTURE
### The Unsurfaced™ creative doctrine · STUDIO v1

Every piece must feel like **evidence that surfaced**, not content that was made.
We are a creative recon group. The work reads like intercepted signal: found,
stamped, filed, leaked. If a piece could have come from a template pack, it dies
in the queue.

## The stance
- We publish **findings**, not opinions. Every take carries a receipt.
- We compete with Wieden+Kennedy on ideas and Playlabs on velocity — never on volume.
- The engine is the production department. The admin is the creative director.
  The kill rate **is** the quality bar: over-generate, shortlist, ship one.

## Visual language — editorial brutalism
- **Type is the image.** Syne (800) does the violence; Space Mono does the filing.
  No third typeface, ever.
- **Palette is law.** Black `#0A0A0A` · Matte `#1A1A1A` · Ivory `#FAF7F2` ·
  Cream `#F5F0E8` · Red `#C41230` (site: `#FF3333`). Red is reserved for the
  found thing — the signal, never decoration.
- **Wire-room furniture** is our ornament: issue numbers, timestamps, evidence
  tags, coordinates, sources printed like receipts, the red dot.
- **The Hand is a character**, not a logo. It judges, reads, reaches, refuses.
  It is rendered from our rig — an asset no one else on earth has.
- Ivory-paper inversions break the black rhythm the way DAILY's paper story does.

## Motion language
- Typographic. Cut on the beat. Holds are confident — minimum 12 frames.
- Type arrives like a stamp or a redaction lifting; it does not "animate in."
- Zero template-pack transitions. No slides, no spins, no particle dust.

## Platform dialects — same substance, three accents
- **LinkedIn**: insight-forward. The finding leads; the caption argues it.
- **IG feed**: stills and carousels. The edition is a dossier — THE SIX.
- **Reels / TikTok**: hook in the first 800ms, take lands by second 4, out by 12.

## Banned moves
Stock gradients. Emoji soup. Engagement-bait phrasing ("you won't believe",
"stop scrolling"). Listicle cadence. Corporate memphis. Drop shadows as taste.
Hashtag walls (five, chosen, or none). Wire photos in outbound work — we render
what we own: type, geometry, the Hand, PLAY-generated art.

## Rituals
- **Daily** (2 minutes): perishable queue — deploy or discard. Never both pieces.
- **Weekly** (the session): durable shelf review, copy passes, download, ledger read.
- **Monthly** (the bench): this month's shelf against best-in-class from the shops
  we respect. Scored without mercy. Anything that looks common retires.
- Formats rotate and retire **before** fatigue, not after.

## Voice
Declarative. Specific. A little dangerous. We name numbers, dates, and sources
**that the finding actually contains** — invention is the one unforgivable move.
We never explain the joke. We write like the reader is smart and busy —
because at this frequency, only the smart and busy are still reading.
